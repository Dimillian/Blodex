# Blodex Art Style Bible

## Style Tag
`painterly-dark-fantasy-v1`

## Core Direction
- Mood: somber medieval-cathedral gloom with embers and worn stone.
- Rendering: painterly concept-art finish with visible brush texture.
- Readability: high silhouette contrast at gameplay scale.
- Perspective: isometric-friendly forms with clear top-plane readability.
- Vanguard anchor: hooded plate knight silhouette with halberd + kite shield.

## Palette
- Primary darks: charcoal, iron blue, damp stone green.
- Warm accents: tarnished gold, ember orange, dried leather.
- Highlight restraint: avoid neon and high saturation.

## Lighting
- Global: low-key, directional top-left fill.
- Accents: subtle rim highlights for interactive entities.
- FX: restrained glows, no bloom-heavy haze.

## Hard Constraints
- Original IP only; no franchise symbols.
- No logos, no watermarks, no text in gameplay sprites.
- Keep backgrounds clean enough for UI readability.

## Prompt Footer (always appended)
`Constraints: original dark fantasy IP; readable silhouette at gameplay scale; no logos or trademarks; no watermark`
